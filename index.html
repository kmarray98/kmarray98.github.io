I am a PhD candidate at the Department of Spatial Economics, Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam and the Tinbergen Institute, supervised by Michael Konig and Ozan Candogan.

I am a fellow in the POPulation-scale social NETwork analysis project at the Institute for Advanced Studies, University of Amsterdam.

I received a Sloan Foundation grant for Mesoeconomics research with Xianglong Kong, Kathryn McDonald, Peter Ohlinger, and Ruochen Dai, and a studentship in Optimisation-Conscious Econometrics.

I hold an MPhil in Economics and Econometrics (cum laude) from the Tinbergen Institute, and a BA in Philosophy and Economics from Oxford.

Interests

Econometrics of networks, economics of networks, machine learning for economics.
